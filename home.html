Bio

My name is Jonathan Probert. I grew up in Maryland and went to Winston Churchill Highschool in Potomac, MD, where I played JV and varsity baseball, and also joined the National Art Honors Society.

I am currently studying computer science at the University of Maryland. I decided to study computer science because of my love for technology. I love the idea of being able to create my own programs and applications that affect how people live day to day. I made this website as a way to learn and practice new HTML and CSS skills as well as provide a way for people to learn a bit more about me.

I do many different things in my free time. Though I have stopped playing baseball, I still enjoy watching the Washington Nationals play every now and then. When I'm not spending time studying or working on school projects I like to hang out with my friends, whether that be in person or online. When I get the chance I also like to spend some time in my school's art studio creating new ceramic pieces. More recently I have been getting into programming, I enjoy learning each new skill as it could help me in the future.
